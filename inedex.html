Immigrants in Pakistan

A brief overview about immigrants living in Pakistan

Immigrants in Pakistan are a significant population, predominantly refugees from neighboring Afghanistan, as well as people from India, Bangladesh, and other countries. Many immigrants live in urban areas like Karachi and contribute to the economy and cultural diversity of the country.

Main Challenges

  • Legal documentation
  • Employment opportunities
  • Access to education
  • Healthcare services
